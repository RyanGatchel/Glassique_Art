Glassique Art

Who are we?

We are Glassique Art! A small glass decorating studio located in Fort Wayne, IN. Specializing in abrasive etching.

What do we do?

Decorate anything glass! We decorate a lot of small things as you will see in our gallery . Trophies, business merchandise, name plates are big sellers. We work with wood as often as we can, because of this we have our own line of wood & glass abominations we call this "Wild Grass Designs." Everyone likes wine (most everyone), and what happens when the bottle is empty? Throw it away you say? NO! give it to us. We can drink the left overs (not really), we recycle the bottles. How you ask? we cut them up, make them into beautiful wind chimes, candle holders, and gift bottles which are available for purchase .
